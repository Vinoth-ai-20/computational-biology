# Module 14 — Papers

| Paper | Type | Why it matters | When to read | Difficulty | Prerequisites |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Alipanahi et al. 2015 — "Predicting the sequence specificities of DNA- and RNA-binding proteins by deep learning" (DeepBind) | Foundational | First major application of CNN to DNA binding prediction; introduced the motif-detector interpretation of CNN filters | Before NB03 | Medium | NB01–02 |
| LeCun, Bengio & Hinton 2015 — "Deep learning" (Nature Review) | Survey | Landmark survey of deep learning; covers CNNs, RNNs, autoencoders; excellent conceptual grounding | Before NB01 | Medium | Module 13 |
| Hochreiter & Schmidhuber 1997 — "Long Short-Term Memory" | Foundational | Original LSTM paper; derives the gate equations; essential reading before NB04 | Before NB04 | High | NB01–03 |
| Vaswani et al. 2017 — "Attention Is All You Need" | Foundational | Original transformer paper; scaled dot-product attention, multi-head attention, positional encoding | Before NB05 | High | NB04 |
| Kingma & Welling 2013 — "Auto-Encoding Variational Bayes" | Foundational | Original VAE paper; ELBO derivation, reparameterization trick | Before NB06 | High | NB01–03 |
| Lopez et al. 2018 — "Deep generative modeling for single-cell transcriptomics" (scVI) | Application | Applied VAE to scRNA-seq; handles count data (negative binomial), batch effects, scalability | After NB06 | Medium-High | NB06 |
| Kipf & Welling 2017 — "Semi-supervised Classification with Graph Convolutional Networks" | Foundational | Original GCN paper; spectral graph convolution simplified to message passing | Before NB07 | High | NB01–03, Module 04 |
| Jumper et al. 2021 — "Highly accurate protein structure prediction with AlphaFold" (Nature) | Foundational | AlphaFold2; evoformer, structure module, recycling; landmark computational biology result | NB11 | Very High | NB05 |

## Key figures

- **Yann LeCun** — CNNs, convolutional architectures
- **Yoshua Bengio** — RNNs, attention, generative models
- **Geoffrey Hinton** — backpropagation, deep belief networks
- **David Kingma** — VAEs, Adam optimizer
- **Brendan Frey** — variational inference in biology
- **Aviv Regev** — single-cell genomics, scVI framework
- **Max Welling** — GCNs, Bayesian deep learning
- **John Jumper** (DeepMind) — AlphaFold2
- **Alexander Rives** (Meta) — ESM protein language models
