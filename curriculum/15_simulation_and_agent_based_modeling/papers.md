# Module 15 — Papers

| Paper | Type | Why it matters | When to read | Difficulty | Prerequisites |
|-------|------|---------------|-------------|------------|---------------|
| Turing (1952) "The chemical basis of morphogenesis" *Phil. Trans. R. Soc. B* | Foundational | Original derivation of Turing instability — reaction-diffusion pattern formation from two interacting morphogens | Before NB03 | Medium | NB01 (ODEs), basic PDE intuition |
| Gillespie (1977) "Exact stochastic simulation of coupled chemical reactions" *J. Phys. Chem.* | Foundational | The SSA algorithm derived from first principles; defines propensity functions | Before NB02 | Medium | Probability, chemical kinetics |
| Kermack & McKendrick (1927) "A contribution to the mathematical theory of epidemics" *Proc. R. Soc. A* | Foundational | Original SIR model derivation; herd immunity threshold; still cited daily in pandemic response | Before NB01 | Low | Basic ODEs |
| Pastor-Satorras & Vespignani (2001) "Epidemic spreading in scale-free networks" *PRL* | Classic | Shows R₀ > 1 always on scale-free networks — shatters herd immunity intuition; 4000+ citations | Before NB07 | Medium | NetworkX basics, SIR model |
| Schelling (1971) "Dynamic models of segregation" *J. Math. Sociol.* | Classic | Original segregation ABM — a canonical example of emergent global pattern from simple local rules | Before NB05 | Low | ABM basics |
| Elowitz et al. (2002) "Stochastic gene expression in a single cell" *Science* | Classic | First measurement of intrinsic vs. extrinsic noise in gene expression; directly motivates Gillespie models | Before NB02 | Medium | Gene regulation basics |
| Potts et al. (2022) "How do animal territories form and change?" *Proc. R. Soc. B* | Review | Modern review of spatial ABM for territorial animal movement; covers random walk, Lévy flights, chemotaxis | Before NB08 | Low | NB05 (ABM basics) |
| Raue et al. (2009) "Structural and practical identifiability analysis of partially observed dynamical models" *Bioinformatics* | Methods | Profile likelihood for ODE parameter identifiability — essential for NB09 | Before NB09 | High | ODE fitting, likelihood basics |
