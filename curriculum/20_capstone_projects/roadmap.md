8. `08_cp03_model_implementation.ipynb` — ODE/network/spatial SIR side-by-side, 20 replicates each

11. `11_phd_application_package.ipynb` — Personal statement template, research proposal, target supervisors
12. `12_month12_readiness_assessment.ipynb` — 50-criterion Track A/B scorecard, action plan generator
