Write a complete short paper (2500–3500 words) describing CP01 (RNA-seq DE analysis):
1. Abstract (200 words, 6-sentence template)

## A02 — PhD research proposal (Track B deliverable)

Write a 1000-word research proposal suitable for EMBL EIPP, IMPRS-LS, or Wellcome Sanger:
1. Title and research question (50 words)
2. Background and motivation (250 words)
3. Proposed approach (400 words): methods, data, tools
4. Expected outcomes (200 words): what you'd find and why it matters
5. Feasibility (100 words): why this is achievable in 3–4 years

The proposal must:
- Be grounded in a real biological question connected to one of the 3 capstone projects
- Cite ≥5 papers from the programme curriculum
- Name at least 2 specific potential supervisors at the target programme
- Overlap with one of the stated target sub-fields (bioinformatics/AI, computational systems biology, molecular life sciences + computation)

Deliverable: `assignments/A02_phd_research_proposal.md` (or `.tex` + PDF).
