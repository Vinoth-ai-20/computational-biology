# Module 06 — References

## Core Books

| Title | Authors | Use |
|-------|---------|-----|
| *Molecular Evolution and Phylogenetics* | Nei & Kumar (2000) | Reference — pull chapters on distance methods, molecular clocks |
| *Principles of Population Genetics* (4th ed.) | Hartl & Clark (2007) | Deep reference — coalescent chapter, selection models |
| *An Introduction to Population Genetics* | Wakeley (2008) | Best modern treatment of the coalescent at working-competence level |
| *Evolution* (4th ed.) | Futuyma & Kirkpatrick (2017) | Broad evolutionary biology reference |
| *Molecular Evolution: A Statistical Approach* | Yang (2014) | Statistical foundations of molecular evolution; Chapters 1–4 are accessible |

## Key Tools (usage-level literacy)

| Tool | What it does | Why relevant |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| MEGA | Distance + parsimony + ML trees | Standard — cited in many papers |
| IQ-TREE | Fast ML tree building + model selection | Current standard for ML phylogenetics |
| BEAST2 | Bayesian phylogenetics + molecular clock | If encountering Bayesian trees in papers |
| PopLDdecay | LD decay visualization | Relevant to GWAS (Module 09) |
| vcftools `--weir-fst-pop` | Fst calculation from VCF | Population differentiation |
