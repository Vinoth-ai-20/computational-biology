# Module 19 — Assignments

This module has one assignment, ongoing throughout the year.

## Assignment 19-A: The Annual Paper-Reading Log

**Due:** Continuously maintained; formally reviewed at Month 6 and Month 12 checkpoints.
**Deliverable:** A completed version of the 52-row reading log from NB12, with all three Pass columns filled, lodged in `paper-notes/` for each logged paper.

### Success criteria

By Month 6:
- At least 20 papers logged in `paper-notes/`
- Pass-3 success rate ≥ 40% (early papers are hard to reconstruct; this is expected to improve)
- At least one journal club walkthrough completed (NB06 format)

By Month 12:
- At least 48 papers logged
- Pass-3 success rate ≥ 65%
- At least one full literature review written (NB07 format, in `assignments/`)
- All 5 NB12 stubs implemented and passing their test harness

## Assignment 19-B: Literature Review

**Scope:** A 1500–2500 word literature review on the sub-field you are converging towards after the Month 6 specialization checkpoint.
**Format:** Markdown, submitted to `assignments/literature_review.md`.
**Structure:** Follow the NB07 framework: scope, search strategy, synthesis tables, gap identification, open questions.
**Track A value:** Demonstrates ability to synthesize a field — directly useful for research application statements.
**Track B value:** A strong literature review is the backbone of a PhD application research statement.
