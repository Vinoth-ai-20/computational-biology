# Module 18 — Assignments

## A01 — Full paper draft: Module 15 simulation study (Tier 2)

**Objective:** write a complete short paper (conference paper length: 2500–3500 words)
describing the epidemic model comparison from Module 15 MP01.

**Structure:**
1. **Abstract** (200 words): apply the 6-sentence template exactly
2. **Introduction** (400–500 words): motivate epidemic modelling, identify the gap
   (homogeneous mixing assumption), state the contribution
3. **Methods** (600–800 words): describe all three models (ODE SIR, network SIR,
   spatial ABM SIR) with sufficient detail for replication; cite relevant papers
4. **Results** (400–500 words): report epidemic curves, peak infection, final size
   for all three models; reference figures directly in text
5. **Discussion** (400–500 words): interpret differences; discuss when the ODE
   approximation fails; limitations; future work
6. **Data and code availability** statement (50 words)
7. **References** (10+ citations in a consistent format)

**Figure requirements:** at least 3 publication-quality figures (from the Module 15 MP01
notebook, re-exported with proper labels, fonts, and colour scheme).

**Deliverable:** `assignments/A01_epidemic_simulation_paper.pdf` (compiled from LaTeX)
+ `assignments/A01_epidemic_simulation_paper.tex`.

## A02 — PhD research proposal (Tier 2)

**Objective:** write a 1000-word research proposal suitable for submission to
an EMBL-EBI or IMPRS PhD programme.

**Structure:**
1. **Title and research question** (50 words)
2. **Background and motivation** (250 words): what is the field, what is the problem,
   why does it matter
3. **Proposed approach** (400 words): what methods you would use, why, what data,
   what computational tools
4. **Expected outcomes and impact** (200 words): what you expect to find and why
   it would advance the field
5. **Feasibility and timeline** (100 words): why this is doable in 3–4 years

**Requirements:**
- Must be grounded in a real biological question you find genuinely interesting
- Must cite at least 5 relevant papers from the programme's 20-module curriculum
- Proposal topic should overlap with one of the explicitly open target sub-fields
  from the project constitution (bioinformatics/AI, computational systems biology,
  molecular life sciences at the interface with computation)

**Deliverable:** `assignments/A02_phd_research_proposal.pdf` + `.tex` source.
