# Module 18 — Papers

Papers in this module serve dual purpose: they are **models to analyse** (structure,
argument, figure design) as well as **content to read**. The "why it matters" column
includes the writing lesson alongside the science.

| Paper | Type | Writing lesson + science | When to read | Difficulty |
|-------|------|--------------------------|-------------|------------|
| Sandve et al. (2013) "Ten simple rules for reproducible computational research" *PLOS Computational Biology* | Methods | The 10-rule template for computational reproducibility; read before NB04 and keep as checklist | Before NB04 | Very low |
| Mensh & Kording (2017) "Ten simple rules for structuring papers" *PLOS Computational Biology* | Methods | Argument structure of a research paper; the C-C-C scheme (context-content-conclusion) in every unit | Before NB01 | Very low |
| Wilson et al. (2017) "Good enough practices in scientific computing" *PLOS Computational Biology* | Methods | Practical reproducibility checklist; data management, project organisation, version control | Before NB04 | Low |
| Rougier et al. (2014) "Ten simple rules for better figures" *PLOS Computational Biology* | Methods | Figure design principles; direct model for NB07 | Before NB07 | Very low |
| Wilkinson et al. (2016) "The FAIR Guiding Principles for scientific data management and stewardship" *Scientific Data* | Foundational | Original FAIR paper; defines all four principles with examples | Before NB08 | Low |
| Fraser et al. (2021) "Preprinting the COVID-19 pandemic" *eLife* | Review | How preprints changed science during COVID; argument for open rapid dissemination; discusses review and trust issues | Before NB08 | Low |
| Bourne et al. (2012) "Ten simple rules for getting published" *PLOS Computational Biology* | Methods | Practical guide to the publication process from submission to final acceptance | Before NB09 | Very low |
| Carey et al. (2020) "Ten simple rules for writing a personal statement for graduate school" *PLOS Computational Biology* | Methods | Template for PhD personal statement; direct input to NB10 | Before NB10 | Very low |
