# Module 07 — Roadmap

10 notebooks, Tier 2 (Working competence), Weeks 9–14

---

## Part 1 — Orientation (Week 9)

- [ ] **NB01** — Why this module, at working-competence depth
  Self-assessment (10 topics), dependency map to Modules 05/09/10/11,
  "what will I be able to do by the end" contract

## Part 2 — Molecular Building Blocks (Weeks 9–10)

- [ ] **NB02** — Amino acids and protein primary structure
  20 amino acids by property, peptide bond, molecular weight from sequence,
  `amino_acid_properties()`, `molecular_weight()`

- [ ] **NB03** — Enzyme kinetics — Michaelis-Menten
  MM equation, Lineweaver-Burk, Km/Vmax interpretation, competitive/non-competitive
  inhibition, Hill equation (cooperativity)

## Part 3 — Central Dogma Mechanics (Weeks 10–12)

- [ ] **NB04** — DNA replication and repair
  Replication fork mechanics, DNA polymerase fidelity (~10⁻⁷ per base),
  repair pathways (BER/NER/MMR), Okazaki fragments, NGS error model connection

- [ ] **NB05** — Transcription and translation mechanics
  Promoter elements, RNA Pol II, splicing (GT-AG rule), ribosome A/P/E sites,
  codon usage bias, connection to RNA-seq junction reads

## Part 4 — Post-translational Regulation (Week 12)

- [ ] **NB06** — Post-translational modifications — survey
  Phosphorylation, glycosylation, ubiquitination, acetylation, methylation;
  kinase cascades, PTM databases (PhosphoSitePlus), connection to Module 10

## Part 5 — Data Literacy (Weeks 13–14)

- [ ] **NB07** — Common biochemistry assay data shapes
  Western blot (band intensity), ELISA (4PL dose-response), gel electrophoresis
  (log MW vs. migration), mass spec (spectrum + peak list), flow cytometry

## Part 6 — Synthesis (Week 14)

- [ ] **NB08** — Mini-project: Michaelis-Menten kinetics simulation
  Portfolio figure — 4 panels from scratch, implemented from memory

- [ ] **NB09** — Research paper reading session
  Three-Pass Method on a biochemistry or molecular biology paper

- [ ] **NB10** — Module assessment
  30-point gate (24/30 = 80%); mock interview question: "explain Km"
