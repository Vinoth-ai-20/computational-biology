# Module 13 — Papers

## Foundational ML Papers

| Paper | Type | Why it matters | When to read | Difficulty | Prerequisites |
|-------|------|---------------|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| Breiman (2001) "Random Forests" — *Machine Learning* | Foundational | Introduced RFs; explains bagging + decorrelation; essential for interview | After NB04 | ★★☆ | Decision trees (NB04) |
| Cortes & Vapnik (1995) "Support-Vector Networks" — *Machine Learning* | Foundational | Original SVM paper; maximum-margin derivation; readable | After NB05 | ★★★ | Linear algebra, NB05 |
| Friedman (2001) "Greedy Function Approximation: A Gradient Boosting Machine" — *Annals of Statistics* | Foundational | GBDT derivation from first principles; explains XGBoost's lineage | After NB07 | ★★★ | Calculus, NB07 |

## Biological ML Application Papers

| Paper | Type | Why it matters | When to read | Difficulty | Prerequisites |
|-------|------|---------------|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| Alipanahi et al. (2015) "Predicting the sequence specificities of DNA- and RNA-binding proteins by deep learning" — *Nature Biotechnology* | Application | DeepBind: first high-impact deep learning paper for genomics binding prediction; introduces sequence-to-function paradigm | After NB13 | ★★☆ | NB13, basics of CNNs (Module 14 preview) |
| Topol (2019) "High-performance medicine: the convergence of human and AI" — *Nature Medicine* | Review/Survey | Authoritative review of ML in clinical medicine; ethical considerations; good for PhD motivation statement | After NB14 | ★☆☆ | NB14 |
| Ghandi et al. (2019) "Next-generation characterization of the Cancer Cell Line Encyclopedia" — *Nature* | Application/Dataset | CCLE: the largest publicly available multi-omics dataset for cancer ML; motivates NB14 and MP01 | Before NB15 | ★★☆ | NB11, NB14 |
| Kircher et al. (2014) "A general framework for estimating the relative pathogenicity of human genetic variants" — *Nature Genetics* | Application | CADD score: ML variant prioritization; shows full ML pipeline on genomic data | After NB13 | ★★☆ | NB09, NB13 |
| Angermueller et al. (2016) "Deep learning for computational biology" — *Molecular Systems Biology* | Review | Best survey of DL methods for biology at the interface of Module 13/14 | After NB15 | ★★☆ | All of Module 13, intro to Module 14 |
